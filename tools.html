Important Hotlines

If you need immediate help, please reach out to one of the following hotlines:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-8255
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA): 1-800-662-HELP (4357)
  • Domestic Violence Hotline: 1-800-799-SAFE (7233)
  • National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA) Helpline: 1-800-931-2237
  • RAINN (Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network): 1-800-656-HOPE (4673) Website
  • National Human Trafficking Hotline: 1-888-373-7888 Website
  • Trevor Project (LGBTQ Youth): 1-866-488-7386 Website
  • GLBT National Help Center: 1-888-843-4564 Website
  • Helping Survivors 1-888-843-4564 Website
