Destress and unwind.

Information

We know it's hard being a student, especially in remote learning. Learn healthy coping mechanisms for when you feel stressed from schoolwork, extracurriculars, or tests.

Activities

View our list of 16 destressing, calming activities, and find one that fits you - or maybe come up with your own, mindful activity. Keep track with our priority to-do list.

Meditate

Use our simple-to-use meditation tool to regulate your breathing and calm yourself during meditation. If you want, you can play our music to set the mood, or use your own!

This resource was created for students by students to help people manage schoolwork-induced stress and unhappiness. School can be challenging, but you can improve your mindset and stay strong with the support of friends, family, and engaging in activities you enjoy.
